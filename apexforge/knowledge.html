Tyres Fitment Pressure
Dyno interpretation

Peak numbers don’t tell the story. Look at the curve shape, consistency, and repeatability. Verify with real-world logs (IAT, timing, AFR, boost, slip).

Watch for: heat soak, torque spikes, boost creep, knock, and inconsistent runs.
Alignment basics

A coilover install without an alignment is unfinished. Understand camber/toe/caster and how they affect tyre wear and turn-in.

Street caution: extreme camber looks aggressive but can reduce braking stability and shred tyres fast.
Compliance & safety
Non-legal advice: know the risk surface before you buy parts.
Read legal notes
Emissions: downpipes/cats, ECU changes, and exhaust noise restrictions vary by region. Always verify local inspection rules.
Safety: incorrect torque, poor fueling, brake fade, or wheel fitment issues can cause catastrophic failures. Use reputable installers and re-check after heat cycles.
